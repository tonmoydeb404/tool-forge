Privacy Policy for Tool Forge

Last updated: June 17, 2025

Tool Forge is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

Data We DO NOT Collect

Tool Forge is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Browsing history or website data
  • Passwords or sensitive credentials
  • Generated passwords, tokens, or any tool outputs
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers

Local Storage Only

Tool Forge operates entirely within your browser using local storage. Any preferences, settings, or temporary data are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers.

How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal data, we don't use, share, or sell any information about you. The extension functions entirely offline for most features, ensuring your data remains private and secure.

Data Security

We implement several security measures to protect your privacy:

  • No External Connections: Most tools work entirely offline
  • Local Processing: All data processing happens on your device
  • Minimal Permissions: We only request necessary browser permissions
  • Open Source: Our code is publicly available for audit

Third-Party Services

Tool Forge may include links to external websites or services (such as our GitHub repository). This privacy policy does not apply to third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

Browser Permissions

Tool Forge requests minimal browser permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your preferences and settings locally
  • Active Tab: To interact with the current tab when needed

We do not request permissions for browsing history, bookmarks, or other sensitive browser data.

Children's Privacy

Tool Forge does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The extension is designed for general productivity use and does not target children specifically.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of Tool Forge after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Open Source Transparency

Tool Forge is an open-source project. You can review our complete source code on GitHub to verify our privacy practices and security implementations.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or Tool Forge's privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: tonmoydeb404@gmail.com
  • GitHub: https://github.com/tonmoydeb404/tool-forge
  • Website: https://tonmoydeb.com

Data Retention

Since we don't collect personal data on external servers, there is no data retention period. Any local settings or preferences are stored on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser data.

Your Rights

As we don't collect personal data, traditional data protection rights (access, rectification, deletion) don't apply. However, you maintain full control over any local data by:

  • Clearing the extension's local storage
  • Uninstalling the extension
  • Reviewing the open-source code

Compliance

Tool Forge is designed to comply with major privacy regulations including:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): EU privacy law
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act): California privacy law
  • PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act): Canadian privacy law

Since we don't collect personal data, most provisions of these regulations don't apply, but we've designed our extension with privacy-by-design principles.

Updates and Notifications

When we update Tool Forge, we may include privacy-related improvements. Users will be notified of significant changes through:

  • Updates to this privacy policy
  • GitHub release notes
  • Extension update notifications (if applicable)

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date stated above and applies to all users of Tool Forge browser extension.
